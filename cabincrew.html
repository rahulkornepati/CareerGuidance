HOW TO JOIN CABIN CREW?



What is Cabin Crew?

Cabin crew consisting of flight attendants carry out the instructions given by pilots or co-pilots outside the cockpit. They are also in charge of the safety and comfort of passengers on a flight, and they inform the passengers when necessary. Male flight attendants are called hosts while female ones are referred to as hostesses. Cabin crew is selected in accordance with the certain rules of aviation organizations and they are also subjected to the rules set by the General Directorate of Civil Aviation. They are in charge of the area from the cockpit door to the rear kitchen area. The cabin crew is not responsible for outside the aircraft or inside the cockpit.



Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Requirement: Below 25 years old.
  • Height: Minimum 5.5 to 5.9 foots.
  • Weight: In proportion to Height
  • Status (Martial): Unmarried
  • Eye Sight: 20/30 vision or better is required.
  • Education: Graduate Degree or 12th/equivalent with three year diploma.
  • Other: No Tattoos, piercings or wild hairstyles.
  • Language: National & one international language

Salary

In India, Flight Steward can earn a salary between Rs. 2.5 lakh to 4.5 Lakh per year as a beginner in the public sector. In Private sector, the salary package is attractive. A Head flight attendant is paid as per their experience, they can earn approximately 6 Lakh per annum. The Cabin Crew member working with the International airlines, they can get salary in between Rs. 9 lakh to 10 lakh per years. Some airlines offer an extra salary for working on off days. In the US, as a fresher, their salary starts from $18,000 per year. Salary is usually varies according to their position and experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum age limit to apply for the position of cabin crew?

The Maximum age to apply for cabin crew position for freshers/ inexperienced (non -cabin crew) candidate is 27 years.

What is the minimum height criteria to be eligible for cabin crew?

The Minimum height criteria is 155 cm.

I do not have customer service experience, can I still apply for cabin crew position?

Previous experience in a customer service role is not a criteria for the cabin crew position.

I was unsuccessful in my interview, can I apply again?

There is no waiting period for candidates who are unsuccessful in the preliminary round / s of interview, however, in case you are unsuccessful in the final round, there is a waiting period of 3 months to reapply for the interview.

Can Male candidates apply for cabin crew?

IndiGo hires only female candidates for cabin crew position, male candidates may apply for any other position as available on our career page.

What are the mandatory documents required at the time of joining?

    You are required to submit the following documents:
  • Passport
  • Pan Card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Mark sheet of Class Xth and XIIth from a recognized Board or University
  • Email/Letter of Relieving/Resignation from the last Employer (to whomsoever applicable)
